Who are we?

Pranav Gupta founder of Liveasy belongs to a trucking background, his father told him about what his father and his customers were going through, from here he got an idea of building an app. Then he did his research and found out that the problem is big, the market is huge and untapped. He believes this is a great opportunity to serve people.

Our mission

Liveasy want to digitize truck booking platform. Trucking is a backbone of every economy not in India around the world but this is highly unorganised there is not so much technology involved in this segment mostly people are connected on phone calls but a phone call is not a problem someone who has load looking for trucks and someone has trucks looking for load are not able to connect efficiently. So we want to organize this industry using technology and we want to empower small-medium businesses such that they are able to generate more revenue, as well as they are able to operate efficiently using technology.As customers are unaware of technology they are following a traditional way to get services that is by approaching brokers or transporters. We want to digitalize the trucking industry, letâ€™s empower SMEs using technology so that they can grow their business.
